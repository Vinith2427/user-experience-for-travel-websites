Golden Temple

Golden Temple is a stunning religious site in Amritsar, Punjab.

Gwalior Fort

Gwalior Fort is a historic fortress located in Madhya Pradesh.

Tawang

Tawang is a beautiful town situated in Arunachal Pradesh.

Camel Safari

Camel Safari in Rajasthan offers a unique and thrilling way to explore the vast Thar Desert, Rajasthan.

Sanchi Stupa

The Sanchi Stupa in Madhya Pradesh is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of India's most important Buddhist monuments located in Madhya Pradesh

Alleppey

Known as the "Venice of the East," Alleppey in Kerala is famous for its picturesque backwaters, houseboats, and tranquil lagoons.

Mehrangarh Fort

Perched on a hill overlooking the city of Jodhpur, Mehrangarh Fort is one of the largest and most majestic forts in India.

Vivekananda Rock Memorial

The Vivekananda Rock Memorial, located off the coast of Kanyakumari, is a spiritual monument dedicated to Swami Vivekananda.

Umngot river

The Umngot River in Meghalaya is famed for its crystal-clear waters, so transparent that boats appear to float on air.

Colva Beach

Colva Beach in Goa is one of the most popular and scenic beaches on the western coast of India.
